About Al-Azeez Quran Academy

At Al-Azeez Quran Academy, we believe that every heart deserves the light of the Quran.

Our goal is to help students of all ages read, understand, and memorize the Quran ‚Äî step by step, with patience and care. We teach 4 days a week, giving enough time to learn without stress.
